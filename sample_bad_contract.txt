Definitions:
This Agreement defines the terms used throughout the contract.

Termination:
Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with no notice, for any reason.

Confidentiality:
Both Parties agree to keep confidential all information forever, even after the contract ends, and the disclosing party may enforce unlimited penalties.

Liability:
Each Party shall be liable for any and all damages, including indirect, consequential, punitive, and speculative losses, without any monetary cap.

Governing Law:
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of a jurisdiction chosen solely by the Provider.

Dispute Resolution:
All disputes shall be resolved exclusively by binding arbitration in the Provider’s home city, with the Provider selecting the arbitrator and bearing all costs.

Payment:
Payments are due immediately upon receipt of an invoice, and late payments will incur a 25% per day penalty.

Intellectual Property:
All IP created during the engagement shall be owned exclusively by the Provider, and the Contractor waives any moral rights.

Data Protection:
Both Parties shall comply with data protection regulations, but the Provider may retain all data indefinitely for any purpose.

Force Majeure:
Performance obligations are suspended for any event, including acts of the other Party, without notice.

Assignment:
The Provider may assign all rights and obligations to any third party without the other Party’s consent.

Notices:
All notices shall be deemed delivered upon posting on the Provider’s website.

Entire Agreement:
This document supersedes all prior discussions, even if those discussions were not included herein.
