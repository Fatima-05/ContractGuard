FULL CONTRACT TEST EXAMPLE 5: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY LICENSING AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Licensing Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into by and between the Licensor and the Licensee.

SECTION 1: LICENSE GRANT [UNHARMFUL]
Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable, worldwide license to use the Licensed Software solely for the purpose of integrating it into Licensee's internal business infrastructure.

SECTION 2: MODIFICATIONS AND DERIVATIVE WORKS [HARMFUL]
Licensee may modify the Licensed Software to ensure compatibility with internal systems. However, any modifications, enhancements, optimizations, or derivative works created by Licensee shall immediately and automatically become the sole and exclusive property of Licensor. Licensee hereby assigns all global rights, titles, and interests in such modifications to Licensor without any right to royalties or compensation.

SECTION 3: ROYALTY PAYMENTS AND REPORTING [UNHARMFUL]
Licensee shall pay Licensor a quarterly royalty fee equal to 5% of the gross revenue derived directly from products utilizing the Licensed Software. Reports shall be submitted within thirty (30) days following the end of each calendar quarter.

SECTION 4: COMPLIANCE AUDITS AND DATA EXPLOITATION [HARMFUL]
Licensor reserves the right to audit Licensee’s systems to verify compliance with royalty tracking. Licensee agrees to provide Licensor's auditors with unrestricted, unannounced physical and remote access to all corporate servers, networks, and databases. Furthermore, during these audits, Licensor may copy and extract any unrelated corporate proprietary records, client files, or trade secrets, and utilize this data for its own commercial purposes without restriction.

SECTION 5: QUALITY CONTROL AND STANDARDS [UNHARMFUL]
Licensee agrees to maintain a high standard of quality in its use of the Licensed Software, ensuring that any integrated products meet or exceed the performance benchmarks outlined in Exhibit B of this Agreement.

SECTION 6: TERMINATION FOR MATERIAL BREACH [HARMFUL]
Either party may terminate this Agreement if the other party commits a material breach and fails to cure it within thirty (30) days of written notice. However, upon any termination initiated by Licensor, Licensee must immediately pay a mandatory liquidating exit fee equal to five (5) years of projected future royalties, calculated based on the highest-earning quarter in the history of this Agreement.

SECTION 7: WARRANTY AND DISCLAIMER [UNHARMFUL]
Licensor warrants that it is the legal owner of the Licensed Software. Except for this express warranty, the software is provided "as-is" without any warranties of merchantability or fitness for a particular purpose.

SECTION 8: DISPUTE RESOLUTION COSTS [HARMFUL]
In the event of legal conflict, the prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney fees. However, Licensee explicitly agrees that prior to filing any formal legal claim, Licensee must deposit a non-refundable retainer bond of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000.00) USD into Licensor’s private corporate legal escrow account to cover Licensor’s preliminary administrative defense costs.
